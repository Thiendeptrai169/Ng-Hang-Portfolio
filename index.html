MAIN QUEST

FEEL

Feel is a healing adventure game about Lino, a lonely boy adrift in the cold vastness of space who has lost all his emotions. With the help of a magical paintbrush, Lino embarks on a journey into his inner world, piecing together forgotten fragments of his feelings. Players don't just accompany Lino to "win," but experience a path of healing — ultimately rediscovering themselves along the way.
